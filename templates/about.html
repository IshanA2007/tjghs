Our Values

The German Honor Society is a club that aims to spread the love of German language and culture around the school. It's not all about learning though; enjoy delicious food, celebrate age-old traditions, and groove along to classics! At GHS, we aren't just learning German culture, we are living it.

Culture
We dive into the heart of German traditions, language, and history.
We're more than a club; we're a family that grows together by sharing experiences and supporting each other.
